Canon PIXMA MG2470 All-in-O Inkjet Printer (White, Grey, InkCartridge)

Key features:

  • USB | USB

Descrption:

Boasting a compact size, the Canon PIXMA MG2470 Inkjet Printer can be easily stored on even small shelves and spaces.
